ZEBRA

Zebras can run upto 65km per hour. Their new born foals can stand after 20 minutes.

  • Scientific name:Equus quagga
  • Family:Equidae
  • Average lifespan:25 years
  • Habitat:Savanna and Temperate grasslands

Zebras' dazzling stripes make them among the most recognisable mammals. Zebras communicates with visuals displays and the flexibility of their lips allows them to make complex facial expressions.
